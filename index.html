How to play

  1. Select a card from your hand.
  2. Click an empty allied slot in one of the three lanes.
  3. Deploy as many cards as your energy allows.
  4. Press Resolve Round. The enemy deploys, then every lane fights.
  5. An unopposed unit damages the opposing leader. Reduce The Blacksite to zero.

Phase Shield: the first incoming hit is ignored.
Mentor: Harrow strengthens your other deployed units.
